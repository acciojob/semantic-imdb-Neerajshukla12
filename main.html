Actors

Leonardo DiCaprio plays Dom Cobb, the main protagonist who is skilled in the art of extraction and inception. DiCaprio's performance is a standout, portraying Cobb's inner turmoil and desire for redemption.

Joseph Gordon-Levitt plays Arthur, Cobb's right-hand man, who helps navigate the complexities of dreams.

Ellen Page (now Elliot Page) portrays Ariadne, the architect of dreams, who designs the intricate dream worlds in which the heist takes place.
