"Inception" is a mind-bending sci-fi thriller directed by Christopher Nolan. It follows Dom Cobb, a skilled thief who steals secrets by entering people’s dreams. Tasked with planting an idea instead of stealing one, he and his team navigate through complex dream worlds full of danger and deception.
