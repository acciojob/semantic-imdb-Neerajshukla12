Summary

Inception is a sci-fi thriller directed by Christopher Nolan. It explores the concept of shared dreaming, where a skilled thief, Dom Cobb, is tasked with planting an idea in someone’s mind — a process known as "inception". The movie blends action, philosophy, and mind-bending visuals.

Actors
